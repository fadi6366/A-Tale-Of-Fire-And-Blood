# Book 1 ending
Arlen will reach home and will get to know Elaria has been sent off because she was a spy. He will then take his horse and follow her to Eldoria. He will ride fast to catch up but when he reaches the port after weeks of travel on the road, all he sees is the ship already left the port. 

# Final sight

Arlen staring at the vessel, as it disappears into the mist.
