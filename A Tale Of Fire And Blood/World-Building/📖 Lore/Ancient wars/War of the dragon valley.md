When David Zevaran the fallen rebelled against his brother Robert Zevaran I because he wanted the throne himself and claimed the lands currently known as [[Dawngard]] as an independent state and no longer under the rule of the [[Zevaran]] tyrants. He would later launch a military campaign from [[Dawngard]] to take the city of [[Zevaragrad The Capital]] from the king of that time, Ultimately failed. 
[[Valmyr]] who were one of the native and one of the most powerful clans of this area fought beside robert zevaran against David the fallen . 
Until then he was David Zevaran but after his defeat he was stripped of the Zevaran name and instead given the name David the fallen , after being humiliated by Robert his head was chopped off and his body fed to the hounds . Carrying the legacy of tyranny Robert ordered to execute 2 sons and 3 daughters of David and his wife along with them to set an example for those to come.
When the rebellion was crushed Robert announced the [[Valmyr]] clan as the ruling family of kingdom of [[Dawngard]] (like a puppet state).
