In 1700 AC when the rebel tribe [[Kantari]] gathered their bannermen and led a 80000 strong host . The battle was fought near the Varak River. The rebels had surrounded the King's 45000 host and cornered them by the river ,until Arlen arrived with 15000 troops as a strategic surpise attack from behind . He executed the dragon split and charged the enemy from 2 sides overwhelming the rebels and ultimately the imperial army won the battle. After the battle Darius III was sent by king theron to finish of those who left in the tribes . That day a massacre by Darius III led to almost 30000 more people dying that day including infants ,children women and old people. [[Kantari]] tribe and their allies were wiped off the map of the world that day.
