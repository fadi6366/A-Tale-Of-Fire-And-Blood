Wife of [[Arkan Zevaran I the Conqueror]]. Said to be the most beautiful woman in the world at that time. Adopted the surname Zevaran after marrying Arkan. She belonged to the Riverlands. Rosavelle travelled to the Ashveil lands for trade with her father and brothers where she caught the eye of Arkan. He fell in love with her and asked her father for her hand. Looking at the young and honorable warrior and of his own good terms with Arkan's father Zevaras Rosavelle's father accepted. It was not long that Rosavelle also fell for Arkan. Later she was taken by one of Arkan's rivals, resulting in the war of the bride, fought by Arkan to liberate the love of his life. They got married the same year in 7BC.
